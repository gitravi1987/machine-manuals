# Section 6 — Operating Instructions

**Machine Name:** [MACHINE_NAME]
**Audience:** Trained production operators

---

> WARNING: Only trained and authorised personnel may operate this machine.
> Read Sections 3, 4, and 5 before operating for the first time.

---

## 6.1 Pre-Start Checks (Every Shift)

- [ ] All guards are in place and secured
- [ ] E-stop buttons are not activated (visual check)
- [ ] Work area is clear of personnel and obstructions
- [ ] Workpiece is correctly loaded
- [ ] Pneumatic pressure is at [X] bar (check gauge)
- [ ] Hydraulic pressure is at [X] bar (check gauge)
- [ ] No visible damage to machine, cables, or hoses
- [ ] Test E-stop function — confirm machine stops

---

## 6.2 Startup Procedure

1. Turn main isolator (LP-1) to ON.
2. Open pneumatic supply valve (LP-2) — confirm pressure builds to [X] bar.
3. Open hydraulic supply valve (LP-3) — confirm pressure builds to [X] bar.
4. Press POWER ON on control panel — confirm HMI displays home screen.
5. Press RESET — confirm no active alarms.
6. Select operating mode: [AUTO / MANUAL / SETUP].
7. Machine is ready for production.

---

## 6.3 Normal Operating Cycle

1. Load workpiece into fixture — confirm correct seating.
2. Ensure all personnel are clear of the working zone.
3. Press CYCLE START — [describe machine operation].
4. Wait for cycle complete signal — [e.g., green light / HMI message].
5. Remove finished workpiece.
6. Inspect part per quality checklist.
7. Repeat from Step 1.

> NOTICE: Do not reach into the working zone during the operating cycle.
> Wait for all motion to stop before unloading.

---

## 6.4 Shutdown Procedure

1. Complete current production cycle.
2. Press STOP on control panel.
3. Wait for all motion to cease.
4. Press POWER OFF on control panel.
5. Close pneumatic supply valve — confirm pressure drops to 0 bar.
6. Turn main isolator (LP-1) to OFF.
7. Clean work area — remove swarf, coolant, and debris.
8. Complete production log sheet.
