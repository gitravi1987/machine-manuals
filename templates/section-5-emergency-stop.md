> Do not use E-stop as a routine stop control.

---

## 5.2 E-Stop Activation

1. Press any E-stop button firmly.
2. Confirm all machine motion has stopped immediately.
3. Do not restart until cause of emergency is identified and resolved.
4. If personnel are injured — call emergency services first.

---

## 5.3 E-Stop Reset Procedure

1. Identify and resolve the cause of the emergency stop.
2. Ensure all personnel are clear of the machine.
3. Rotate E-stop button clockwise to release (or pull to release — confirm type on machine).
4. Reset control system via RESET button on panel.
5. Perform test cycle before resuming production.

> NOTICE: E-stop must be reset manually. It does not auto-reset.
