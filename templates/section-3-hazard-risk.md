## 3.1 Risk Assessment Methodology

Risk assessment conducted per ISO 12100:2010:
- Hazard identification by zone and task
- Risk estimation: Severity (S) x Probability (P) = Risk Level (R)
- Risk reduction hierarchy: Elimination > Safeguarding > Information

| Level | Severity | Probability | Action |
|-------|----------|-------------|--------|
| HIGH | Serious injury or death | Likely | Immediate action required |
| MEDIUM | Moderate injury | Possible | Action required |
| LOW | Minor injury | Unlikely | Monitor |
